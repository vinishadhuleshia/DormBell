About

Our Goal

Our project is a dorm alert system where the user gets notified on their device when someone either knocks or is close to their door. It could be used in such situations where the student cannot hear the knocking or if the student is not present in their room. It can help students be aware of their surroundings and ensure safety especially on campuses where dorms open directly outside the building. In the future, in scenarios where the student may feel unsafe for example late at night or while other students surrounding them are home for break, having a camera implemented outside their door will provide additional security for the student.

Product

An example of a situation where this project could be implemented is if the student is in their dorm room, they have headphones on and cannot hear someone knocking on their door, this project alerts the user that someone is outside. Another example is if the student is not in their room but someone knocks on the door, by using a distance sensor we are able to trigger a notification alerting the user that a person is within a certain range outside of their door.
